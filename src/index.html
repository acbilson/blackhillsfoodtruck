Black Hills Food Truck (BHFT) Network

Mission Statement

Problem: Black Hills locals don't know what food trucks operate near them or where they'll be stopped on a given day.

Solution: Pull together relevant food truck information, such as menus and stops, to one place available to anyone, anywhere.
